About

Gabrielle is an aerialist, contortionist, painter, and technologist who lives to make meaningful and beautiful art. Her style is graceful and vibrant with a whimsical and playful spirit.

Fabric

Gabrielle's style on Aerial Silks integrates contortion flexibility with exciting dynamic skills, sure to amaze any audience.

Lyra

Aerial Hoop is an apparatus made for contortionists. Gabrielle blends a style of flowy, twisty, and weavy transitions to create an elegant and intriguing visual experience.

Contortion

Gabrielle blends her background in Mongolian contortion, Dance, and Physical Theater to create delightful and elegant groundwork. Unlike aerial which requires a stable rigging system, Contortion can be performed almost anywhere, with minimal setup.
